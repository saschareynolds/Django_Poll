Help us choose what ideas to pursue!



Here are our options:

1. Find a buddy platform.

This would connect students to others working on the same problem set.

5C Rideshare, but for homework!



2. Group Order.

A platform that helps people order in groups.

Individuals can pay for something and then that item would be added to the order.



3. Wheel Share.

A platform where renters can rent their wheels to the riders.

Ofo, but you can add your own wheels and get paid per ride.



4. Voice Recognition.

Could be used either for ordering at restaurants

(no need to have someone working the drivethrough orders) or over the phone orders,

or, for taking down stats for sports games by just talking to your phone
